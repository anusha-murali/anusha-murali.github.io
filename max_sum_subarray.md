### 1. Maximum Sub Subarray

***

Find the contiguous subarray of a given array $A$ such that it has the largest possible sum.

> **Example**: $A = [5, -2, 4, -10,  1, 7, -9, 8]$. The maximum sum subarray of $A$ is $[1, 7]$ and the maximum sum is 8.
